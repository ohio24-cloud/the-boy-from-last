ABOUT THE STORY

What is The Boy from Last?

This is an ongoing story presented chapter by chapter — a space where each page unfolds slowly, carrying atmosphere, silence, memory, and the unease of being watched or understood too closely.

The world of this story is inspired by tension, curiosity, loneliness, and the fragile line between fiction and reality.

Adapted from Spanish play: "El chico de la última fila" not a fully Based on it..

CHARACTER INTRODUCTION

People in the Story

Every story is carried by the people inside it. These are the first shadows from The Boy from Last.

Floyy

*Main Character

A 17-year-old high school student who sits in the back row. Seemingly quiet and detached, he is incredibly intelligent, observant, and subtly manipulative. He begins writing a serial story about his classmate's family, creating a psychological web of fiction and reality.

Prof. Jenn

*The One Who Notices

A 55-year-old frustrated literature teacher who acts as Claudio’s mentor and eager reader. His initial desire to improve Floyy's writing evolves into a dangerous, addictive obsession with Floyy's voyeuristic narratives.

Selen

*Professor Jenn's wife

She is therapist and Professor Jenn's wife.

Peter

*Floyy's Friend

Floyy’s classmate, an average, sports-loving teenager who befriends Floyy, completely unaware that he and his family are characters in Floyy's unfolding drama.

Pedro Em

*Novelist

A traditional, high-class father whose life becomes the primary subject of Floyy's stories.

Merissa

*Peter's Mother

Prof. Steve

*professor Jenn's closest friend and colleague.
