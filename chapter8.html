Entry One — The Phone Call Overheard

The next day... Professor Jenn resumed Floyy's special class as usual.

The classroom remained unusually quiet.

Floyy looked toward Jenn and finally broke the silence.

"Sir..."

"That boy..."

"...was Peter."

Jenn immediately looked up.

"...What?"

Floyy nodded.

"Last night, while I was passing by his room, I heard him talking to someone on the phone."

Jenn leaned forward.

"What exactly did you hear?"

Floyy took a deep breath.

"He sounded terrified."

"And he kept repeating the same thing..."

"Listen to me..."

"Dad killed someone..."

"I'm not lying..."

"He actually killed someone..."

Jenn remained silent for a moment. Then slowly said,

"Floyy..."

"Maybe you misunderstood."

"Perhaps it was just your imagination."

Floyy calmly shook his head.

"No, sir."

"I observed everything very carefully."

Peter's Room — Evidence

Jenn looked at him seriously.

"...Did you really see that torn book?"

Floyy nodded.

"Yes."

"His entire dustbin was filled with torn pages."

Floyy paused. Then quietly added—

"But sir... they weren't random pages."

Jenn's head slowly lifted.

"What do you mean?"

"The torn pages..." Floyy's voice became lower. "...they had author names on the spine."

"I recognized one of them."

Jenn waited.

"Pedro."

Jenn froze.

"Peter was tearing apart... his own father's novels."

Jenn lowered his head.

"No..."

"No..."

"Tell me everything again."

"From the beginning."

"We shouldn't rush."

"Suspecting Peter without certainty would be unfair."

Floyy quietly continued.

"His behaviour has become stranger than before."

"He doesn't even talk to me properly anymore."

"Yesterday evening, I went to call him for dinner."

"I told him..."

"Come on, let's eat."

"We still have coding to finish."

He suddenly shouted at me...

"To hell with your coding!"

"Leave me alone."

"And don't enter my room again."

Floyy paused.

"After some time, Marissa somehow convinced him to come downstairs."

"We were all having dinner."

The Dinner Table

Pedro looked at Peter and asked...

"Why aren't you eating?"

Before Peter could answer... Marissa quietly said,

"If Florence had cooked today..."

"He would've finished everything."

The moment those words left her mouth...

Pedro glared at her.

"What nonsense are you talking about?"

Marissa immediately fell silent.

A few seconds later she whispered,

"I shouldn't have said that in front of Peter."

"Florence's death had affected Peter more than anyone."

A few moments later... Marissa looked toward Pedro.

"They found Florence's family."

"They finally found her father."

"But..."

"They refused to perform her funeral."

"Her only close relative..."

"Her elder sister..."

"She had already died last year because of an illness."

Pedro quietly asked,

"So..."

"What will happen now?"

Marissa replied,

"We'll probably have to ask the church volunteers to collect money for her funeral."

Pedro interrupted.

"That won't be necessary."

"I'll pay for it."

Marissa looked surprised.

"...You will?"

Pedro nodded.

"She worked in our house for a long time."

"It's the least I can do."

"And the funeral shouldn't be delayed."

"Keeping the dead waiting..."

"...is disrespectful."

Peter silently stared at Pedro throughout the conversation.

His eyes...

Were filled with something impossible to describe.

Without saying another word...

He stood up.

"My dinner is over."

And walked back toward his room.

Jenn suddenly clenched his fist.

"That bastard..."

"That filthy man..."

Floyy looked at him.

"...Sir?"

Jenn slowly calmed himself.

"But whenever I see Pedro at school..."

"He looks perfectly normal."

"He laughs with everyone."

"He plays basketball."

"He behaves as though nothing has happened."

Floyy quietly replied,

"The moment he leaves school..."

"...he becomes a completely different person."

Library — Evidence Appears

The following day...

Professor Jenn and Floyy met inside the school library.

Floyy unlocked his phone.

Then showed several CCTV screenshots.

"I somehow convinced Jessic to get these."

Jenn carefully examined every image.

One photograph immediately caught his attention.

A masked boy...

Standing outside the hotel...

Talking to someone.

Jenn's eyes widened.

He smiled.

"...Found you."

"...So it really was you..."

"...Peter."

He looked genuinely excited.

"Now we only need to know..."

"...how much Peter actually knows."

Jenn looked toward Floyy.

"...What happened?"

"Why do you look so upset?"

Floyy quietly lowered his head.

"I used to be jealous of Peter."

"He had everything..."

"Everything I could only dream of."

He paused.

"But now..."

"I can't even bear to look at him."

Jenn gently smiled.

"We're trying to help him."

"If Peter tells us the truth..."

"...then we'll help him."

Floyy nodded.

Then quietly said,

"This Friday..."

"Peter's elder sister..."

"Denish..."

"...is coming home for Mr. Pedro's birthday."

Jenn immediately looked at him.

"...Pedro's birthday?"

Floyy nodded.

"Peter tells everything only to Denish."

"She's the only person he truly talks to."

Jenn thought for a moment.

Then leaned closer.

"Listen carefully."

"You need to hear every conversation."

"But don't get caught."

"I'm sure..."

"We'll discover something important."

"And that..."

"...is your next assignment."

Floyy smiled.

The two quietly bumped fists.

A new phase of the investigation had officially begun.

The Investigation Continues...

A phone call revealing murder. Torn pages in a dustbin. A masked boy in a CCTV frame.
Peter knows something. And this Friday, when his sister Denish arrives,
the truth may finally emerge from behind closed doors.
