Chapter Ten

Not Peter

CASE FILE NO. 10 — IDENTITY UNCONFIRMED

"Every secret leaves behind a fragment. The question is... who dares to put them together?"

◆Psychological Fiction / Revelation

◆Obsession / Deception / A Face Behind the Mask

Entry One — The Parking Lot

The following day...

As Professor Jenn wrapped up Floyy's special class, he picked up his bag and said,

"Whatever happened yesterday..."

"Write everything in your assignment."

"And submit it tomorrow."

Jenn walked toward the parking lot.

Just as he unlocked his car...

His phone rang.

"Sir... you remember today's meeting, right?"

"It starts at 3 PM."

"The Academic Society Chairman was asking about you as well."

Jenn closed his eyes for a second.

"...Yeah."

"I'm on my way."

The Garden of Autumn Flowers

At that very moment...

He noticed Merissa.

She was standing a few cars away, talking to Pedro over the phone.

"I was already nearby..."

"So I thought..."

"Why don't we have lunch together?"

"I'll be there in a few minutes."

Jenn froze.

Without thinking...

He got inside his car.

As Merissa drove away...

Jenn quietly followed her.

A few minutes later...

Merissa stopped near a small garden filled with autumn flowers.

She stepped out of her car.

Instead of leaving...

She simply stood there.

Watching the flowers sway in the wind.

Jenn remained inside his car.

Unable to look away.

He whispered to himself...

"...It was you."

"...You were Wren."

"...Wren Merissa..."

His phone suddenly rang again.

Reality pulled him back.

The University Hallway

Jenn finally reached the university where the meeting was being held.

Merissa had arrived there as well.

She stood silently, admiring a painting displayed inside the department hallway.

Jenn slowly walked toward her.

For the first time in years...

He gathered the courage to speak.

But before he could...

Pedro arrived.

The two immediately started laughing.

They admired the painting together.

Then began discussing what they should order for lunch.

Moments later...

They walked away together.

Jenn simply stood there.

Watching them disappear.

Once again...

He couldn't say a single word.

He left the building.

Lit a cigarette.

And under his breath...

Started cursing Pedro.

Entry Two — The Envelope

That night...

Jenn lay awake on his bed.

Restlessly turning from one side to the other.

Muttering words even he couldn't understand.

Just then...

Selen entered the room.

"What are you mumbling about?"

She smiled softly.

"Want some wine?"

"My clients gifted me a bottle today."

"It was our last therapy session."

She sat beside him.

"Sometimes..."

"Talking honestly is enough to reduce the distance between two people."

After a pause she asked,

"You don't take those extra classes anymore?"

Jenn nodded.

"I told you."

"There won't be any more classes."

Selen looked guilty.

"I wanted to apologize."

"For that night..."

"When we went out for dinner."

"I shouldn't have said those things."

Jenn smiled faintly.

"What are you talking about..."

Before he could finish...

His phone vibrated.

He immediately stood up.

"I'll be back."

Selen watched him walk toward the other room.

"Where are you going?"

Jenn had already dialed Floyy's number.

"Floyy."

"What exactly did you send me?"

"Whose photograph is this?"

Floyy replied,

"It isn't the photo that's important, sir."

"It's the letter."

"A strange letter arrived this morning."

Jenn remained silent.

Floyy continued.

Floyy's Account

"This morning... Merissa handed an envelope to Mr. Pedro."

"She said... 'Maybe it's for you. Your name is written on it.'"

"I walked toward them. Merissa handed me the grocery bags."

"She asked me to put everything inside the refrigerator."

"I casually asked Mr. Pedro... 'Whose letter is it?'"

"He simply replied... 'Just a letter.'"

"But something felt wrong. The moment he saw that envelope... his hands started trembling."

"He was so disturbed... that he accidentally dropped the wine bottle."

Jenn frowned.

"What happened after that?"

Floyy continued.

Floyy's Account — continued

"A little later... I entered his room. He wasn't inside."

"He was sitting alone in the garden. I searched his coat."

"I thought he'd kept the letter there. But... it wasn't there."

"I looked outside again. He was no longer in the garden. He was coming back."

"I quickly hid behind the bookshelf inside his room."

"Mr. Pedro entered. He sat beside his desk. Opened a drawer. And took out the letter."

"He read it carefully. Then... he fed the entire letter into the paper shredding machine."

"After that... he picked up a book. And walked back toward the garden."

Jenn asked,

"...Then?"

Floyy replied,

"The moment he left... I collected every shredded piece."

"I brought them home."

"And somehow pasted them together."

Jenn held his breath.

"What was written on it?"

Floyy slowly answered,

"I have the CCTV footage."

"Meet me at the park tonight."

"11:00 PM."

Jenn immediately called him.

"CCTV footage?"

Floyy replied,

"Yes."

Jenn whispered,

"I think... it could be Peter."

"He had already mentioned something about the hospital to his sister."

Floyy nodded.

"Maybe."

"But... something else happened today."

Jenn immediately asked,

"What?"

Floyy continued.

Entry Three — Pizza, and a Cancelled Movie

"Tonight we were having pizza."

"Merissa noticed Mr. Pedro sitting quietly."

"She smiled and asked..."

"Why are you so upset?"

Pedro replied... "Nothing happened."

Merissa suggested... "Why don't we all go for a movie? Or maybe a long drive after dinner?"

Pedro refused. "I can't. Mr. Kim suddenly called me. You three go and enjoy yourselves."

At that moment...

Peter quietly stood up.

"I also have to meet a friend."

Merissa cancelled the plan.

Floyy lowered his voice.

"Sir... I don't know why... but Peter looked different today."

"I think... I'm going to the same park mentioned in that letter."

Jenn immediately became serious.

"You mean... you're going there right now?"

"Without discussing it with me?"

Floyy smiled nervously.

"If I see them meeting... we'll finally know something."

Jenn remained silent for a second.

Then said,

"Fine. Go. But don't get caught."

Assignment — Continuation

Filed by Floyy — Submitted the Following Morning

The Assignment, In His Own Words

I reached the park before 11 PM.

I stood far away... hidden behind the trees. The entire park was almost empty.

After a few minutes... I saw Mr. Pedro enter the park. He looked extremely nervous.

He kept checking his watch. And repeatedly looked around, as if he was waiting for someone.

Then... from the opposite side of the park... a masked man slowly started walking toward him.

It was the same masked person I had seen in the CCTV photographs from Hotel Bellavista.

My heartbeat became faster. "Is that Peter?"

I slowly moved closer. But before they could even meet... Mr. Pedro's phone suddenly rang.

He looked at the screen. His face instantly turned pale.

Without saying a single word... he ran out of the park.

The masked man stood there for a few seconds... then quietly turned around and started walking away.

I immediately chased him. I shouted...

"Peter!"

"Peter... stop!"

The Assignment — Final Lines

He didn't stop. I caught up to him. And pulled off his mask...

The moment I saw his face... I froze.

It wasn't Peter.

Assignment Ends.

A shredded letter promising footage. A midnight meeting broken by a ringing phone.
A mask torn away by trembling hands — and a face that was never supposed to be there.
If it wasn't Peter behind the mask... then who has been watching all along?
